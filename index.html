Undergraduate Researcher // ISAACS

UC Berkeley Vive Center for Enhanced Reality

Co-leading team of undergraduate researchers developing ISAACS, the Immersive Semi-Autonomous Aerial Command System. We remotely operate drones outdoors for mapping, radiation detection, and more all through our virtual reality interface integrating flight controls and real-time sensor data visualization. Our paper was accepted and will appear at IEEE Nuclear Science Symposium and Medical Imaging Conference. Check out the ISAACS website: ISAACS

Undergraduate Researcher

Berkeley Deep Drive

At DeepDrive I designed and wrote the software that connected all components of our autonomous scale-model car. From sensor input to neural network inference, and back to PWM motor commands and remote controller input processing, the ROS-based software ran on our Nvidia TX2 and interfaced with an Arduino. Once the car was up and running I collected training data and trained our Pytorch implementation of SqueezeNet, an efficient CNN. Check out the repo here Autonomous Scale Model Car
